Spanish Immersion

  • Information
  • Requirements
  • Questions

The spanish immersion program, takes a focus on children whose parents speak spanish as their native language, and are looking for their children to be able to learn spanish and english at the same time. This way the child will not just learn english, but will also continue developing it's spanish speaking skills while also learning the basics of being in school in preparation before entering kindergarden.

The age requirement for the child to be enrolled in the program is:

  • Children age must be between 18 months to 5 years
