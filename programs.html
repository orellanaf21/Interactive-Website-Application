Programs

The early childhood program offers two programs. The spanish immersion program and also a bilingual options programs. Based on what is best for your child, each of the programs is a good development experience.

Below is the information for each of the programs that are currently being offered.

The spanish immersion program, focuses on children from parents that are from spanish speaking parents or parents looking for their kids to learn spanish at an early age.

The bilingual options program, is for children that are from parents that speak a second language and would like their child to continue speaking that language alongisde learning english as well.
