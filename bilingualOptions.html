Bilingual Options

  • Information
  • Requirements
  • Questions

The bilingual options program, is for parents who are looking that their children can continue learning the native language that their parents speak while also learning english, and also learning the basics of being in school in preparation of kindergarden. If you want your children to continue developing skills in their parents native language then this program is for them.

Note that the program offer bilingual options for the following languages: Portuguese, French, German, and Chinese.

The age requirement for the child to be enrolled in the program is:

  • Children age must be between 18 months to 5 years
